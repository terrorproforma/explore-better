One workspace / two operators

Your AI sees—and safely operates—the folder you mean.

Explore Better turns the visible pane, tabs, selection, and authorized roots into structured local context and bounded actions. Your AI can find, inspect, compare, navigate, and reveal results without screen scraping or a general-purpose shell.

  • Read-only firstStart with discovery and analysis. Writes stay absent until you explicitly add them.
  • Exact rootsEach client receives its own folders, tools, limits, and revocable profile.
  • Visible resultsThe AI can reveal its answer back in the pane you are already looking at.
